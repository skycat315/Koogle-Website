KOOGLE

What is KOOGLE?

Koogle is a peanut butter spread which is first released in the USA in 1971.

This sweetened peanut butter came in four different flavours: vanilla, chocolate, cinnamon, and banana.

You will love the very unique and smooth texture.

Koogle Peanut Butter Spread - 1970's?

How Koogle is Made

1. Grow the delicious peanuts

The peanuts are grown in our own farm. We plant the seeds in late spring and carefully grow them until they are harvested in fall.

2. Make the peanuts butter

Put the peanuts in the processor and stir slowly. Taking time slowly will make the butter smooth and creamy.

3. Season each butter

Mix it with vanilla, chocolate, cinnamon and banana respectively. Each of them is from our partner farmers around the world.

4. Fill in a jar

The freshly made peanut butter is sealed in a jar. We keep the butter fresh until the moment you open it for the first time.
