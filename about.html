Our History

  • 1903
    Kraft grew out of a wholesale cheese-delivery business established in Chicago in 1903 by James L. Kraft.
  • 1909
    His three brothers later joined the firm, and in 1909 it was incorporated as J.L. Kraft Bros. & Company.
  • 1930
    The company was acquired by National Dairy Products Corporation.
  • 1971
    We introduced Koogle for the first time.
  • 1976
    The company name was changed to Kraft, Inc.
  • 1981
    Although Koogle was loved by many people, we discontinued producing it.
  • 2015
    We merged with H.J. Heinz Holding Corporation to become a division and brand of The Kraft Heinz Company, one of the world's largest food and beverage manufacturers.
